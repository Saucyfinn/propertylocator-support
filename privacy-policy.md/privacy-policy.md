# PropertyView Privacy Policy

_Last updated: 4 January 2026_

PropertyView (“the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

---

## Information We Collect

PropertyView does **not** require user accounts and does **not** collect personally identifiable information such as names, email addresses, or contact details.

The App may access the following information solely to provide core functionality:

- **Location data**  
  Used to display indicative property boundaries, overlays, and mapping information relative to your device’s GPS position.

Location data is:
- Used only while the app is in use
- Not stored on our servers
- Not shared with third parties

---

## Use of Location Information

Location information is used to:
- Display map position
- Align indicative property boundaries and overlays
- Improve usability of location-based features

Location accuracy may vary depending on device hardware, signal quality, and environmental factors.

---

## Data Storage and Sharing

PropertyView:
- Does **not** sell user data
- Does **not** share personal data with third parties
- Does **not** store location history
- Does **not** track users across apps or websites

Map and property data may be sourced from third-party public or licensed datasets and is displayed for **informational purposes only**.

---

## Third-Party Services

The App may use Apple-provided services such as:
- Apple Maps
- iOS location services

These services are governed by Apple’s own privacy policies.

---

## Legal Disclaimer

All property boundaries, overlays, and location-based information displayed in the App are **indicative only**.  
They are **not legally binding** and must not be relied upon for determining legal property boundaries. Users should seek professional advice for boundary verification.

---

## Children’s Privacy

PropertyView does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.
